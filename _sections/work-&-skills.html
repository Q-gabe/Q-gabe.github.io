I have worked as an intern in Changi General Hospital as a Microbiology Lab Technician alongside the microbiology lab team there for an academic year in polytechnic. Together with my FYP partner, I also did my Final Year Thesis, doing rigorous lab work and subsequent data analysis of the antimicrobial resistances of hypermutagenic strains of Pseudomas aeruginosa from various samples taken from patients suffering from chronic lung diseases. Working together and receiving guidance from a team of highly trained lab techs and supervisors made the experience one of the most enriching experiences I ever had. The study was subsequently presented at the CGH/EHA Annual Scientific Meeting in 2014. You can access the paper here.

My coding proficiencies include C, Java, Python, JavaScript and HTML5. I aim to eventually achieve the skills required for full-stack development and other software design.
